Singapore Trip Itinerary

🛫 Things to Prepare Before Coming to Singapore by Flight

  • Visa Requirements: Malaysian citizens do not need a visa for short visits to Singapore (up to 30 days for tourism). Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry. Submit an SG Arrival Card online via the ICA website or MyICA app within 3 days before arrival (free, quick process). More info: ICA Visa Requirements, MFA Singapore.
  • Credit Card: Enable your Mastercard (or Visa) for overseas transactions via your bank's app or customer service to avoid declines. Singapore widely accepts contactless payments, but confirm no foreign transaction fees with your bank.
  • Cash: Bring 100-200 SGD in small denominations for hawker centers, public transport top-ups, or areas with limited card acceptance. Exchange at Malaysian airports or money changers for better rates than in Singapore. Consider using Wise for transfers.
  • Roaming Data: Activate a roaming plan from your Malaysian telco for seamless data in Singapore. Options for a 3-day trip: Check your telco or use Wise for related financial advice.

📅 Day 1: Sunday, July 27, 2025
🕘 Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM) ✈️ Arrival & Dim Sum Breakfast 🥟 126 Dim Sum Wen Dao Shi, 126 Sims Ave, Geylang
Land at Changi 9 AM, Grab (~S$10, 10 min) or MRT to Aljunied. Siew mai, congee (~S$15-20/person). 24/7 spot, lively.
🛎️ Hotel Check-In & Rest 🏨 Central hotel (e.g., City Hall/Marina Bay)
MRT (~30 min from Aljunied). Check-in, relax ~1-2 hours to recharge post-flight.
🕛 Afternoon (12:00 PM - 6:00 PM) 🌿 Gardens by the Bay & Marina Bay Sands 🏞️ Gardens by the Bay (MRT: Bayfront); Marina Bay Sands
MRT to Bayfront. Visit Cloud Forest dome (S$28 combo ticket, ~1.5 hours)—indoor waterfall, misty walkways. Stroll to MBS: Browse The Shoppes (luxury retail, free), ArtScience Museum (S$23, ~1 hour; tech/creativity exhibits).
🕕 Evening (6:00 PM - 10:30 PM) 🌅 Marina Bay Stroll & Dinner 🚶 Marina Bay Promenade; Jyu Lae Bistro, Suntec City
Easy ~1 km walk for Merlion views. Taiwanese dinner with live music (~S$30-50/person, reserve). Near Promenade MRT.
🎡 Singapore Flyer Ride 🎠 Singapore Flyer
Walk ~5 min, ride ~9 PM (S$40/adult, 30 min). Night skyline views. Book via Klook/on-site. Hotel by 10:30 PM.
📅 Day 2: Monday, July 28, 2025
🕘 Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM) 🏛️ National Museum of Singapore 🖼️ National Museum (MRT: Bras Basah)
MRT (~10 min). Self-guided tour (S$15/adult, ~1-2 hours). History exhibits on Singapore’s economic rise. Air-conditioned.
🕛 Afternoon (12:00 PM - 6:00 PM) 🏮 Chinatown & Maxwell Lunch 🥢 Chinatown (MRT: Chinatown); Maxwell Food Centre
MRT/walk (~10 min). Buddha Tooth Relic Temple (free), shophouses. Lunch at Maxwell (chicken rice ~S$5-10/person).
🕕 Evening (6:00 PM - 9:00 PM) 🚤 Singapore River Cruise & Dinner ⛵ Boat Quay; Clarke Quay
Walk (~5 min) to Boat Quay for bumboat cruise (S$25/adult, 40 min, evening). Riverside dinner at Clarke Quay (seafood/satay ~S$20-40/person). Hotel by 9 PM.
📅 Day 3: Tuesday, July 29, 2025
🕗 Morning (8:00 AM - 11:00 AM) 🍜 Breakfast & Walk in Geylang 🥙 Geylang Serai New Market; Geylang area
MRT (~20 min to Aljunied/Paya Lebar). Nasi lemak/popiah at hawker (~S$5-10/person). Walk ~1 hour: Wet market, Sri Sivan Temple. Shaded paths.
🕚 Afternoon (11:00 AM - 3:00 PM) 🛍️ Orchard Road Shopping & Lunch 🏬 Orchard Road (MRT: Orchard)
MRT (~20 min). Shop ION Orchard (luxury/souvenirs). Food court lunch (laksa/dim sum ~S$10-15/person). Relax until ~3 PM.
🕒 Evening (3:00 PM - 7:00 PM) 🛫 Departure ✈️ Changi Airport (Jewel Changi)
MRT/Grab (~30 min, arrive 5 PM). Explore Jewel’s Rain Vortex (free). Depart 7 PM flight.
