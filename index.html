üõ´ Things to Prepare Before Coming to Singapore by Flight

  • Visa Requirements: Malaysian citizens do not need a visa for short visits to Singapore (up to 30 days for tourism). Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from the date of entry. Submit an SG Arrival Card online via the ICA website or MyICA app within 3 days before arrival (free, quick process). More info: ICA Visa Requirements, MFA Singapore.
  • Credit Card: Enable your Mastercard (or Visa) for overseas transactions via your bank's app or customer service to avoid declines. Singapore widely accepts contactless payments, but confirm no foreign transaction fees with your bank.
  • Cash: Bring 100-200 SGD in small denominations for hawker centers, public transport top-ups, or areas with limited card acceptance. Exchange at Malaysian airports or money changers for better rates than in Singapore. Consider using Wise for transfers.
  • Roaming Data: Activate a roaming plan from your Malaysian telco for seamless data in Singapore. Options for a 3-day trip: Check your telco or use Wise for related financial advice.
