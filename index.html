🛫 Things to Prepare Before Coming to Singapore by Flight

  • Visa Requirements: Malaysian citizens do not need a visa for short visits (up to 30 days for tourism). Ensure passport is valid for 6 months. Submit SG Arrival Card online via ICA website or MyICA app within 3 days before arrival (free). More info: ICA Visa Requirements, MFA Singapore.
  • Credit Card: Enable Mastercard/Visa for overseas transactions via bank app or customer service. Confirm no foreign transaction fees. Singapore accepts contactless payments widely.
  • Cash: Bring 100-200 SGD in small denominations for hawker centers, transport top-ups. Exchange at Malaysian airports or money changers for better rates. Consider Wise for transfers.
  • Roaming Data: Activate roaming plan from Malaysian telco for seamless data. Check telco options for a 3-day trip or use Wise for financial advice.
